Laboratory Objectives

UNNC-FAB is a key experimental platform supported by the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) and the city’s first Fab micro-assembly laboratory meeting international Fablab standards. The lab is dedicated to exploring the future trends in product design and advanced manufacturing, focusing on core digital fabrication technologies such as 3D printing, CNC machining, electronic circuit design, embedded programming, laser cutting, and mechatronics integration. It aims to build a creative-driven, practice-centered, and future-oriented integrated teaching and research platform.

  • Integrating Teaching and Practice
  • Advancing Frontier Research
  • Promoting Industry-Academia Collaboration
  • Advocating Sustainable Innovation
  • Building an Interdisciplinary Platform
